Welcome to the Experience

On Wednesday, February 11, 2026, we will host a Shadow Day for prospective students alongside a Parent Experience, both taking place at The Ritz-Carlton Dorado Beach. Together, these experiences are designed to provide a firsthand experience for how Alpha delivers on its commitments every day.

✨ About Shadow Day

A Shadow Day is the next step for families who have applied or submitted a deposit for Alpha Dorado. Students will participate in a guided experience that reflects a real Alpha school day, including:

Personalized, mastery-based academics supported by Alpha’s AI-powered learning model

A collaborative life skills workshop led by Alpha guides

A learning environment built on high standards, high support, and student motivation

This experience allows students to connect the Alpha model to how school will feel day to day, while also giving our guides the opportunity to evaluate potential fit.
